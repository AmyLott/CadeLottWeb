About

Hey there, I’m Cade, a freelance writer. When I’m not enjoying the outdoors, I’m reading a horror novel or transforming the world one sentence at a time.

Like most writers, I fantasize of constructing an unputdownable novel worthy of comparison with geniuses like the King, Straub, or Jackson (Shirley).

I won my first international literary competition at fourteen; I gained my first credit as a published writer later the same year.

While you’re here, I hope you’ll scroll through some of my blogs and/or short stories. Tell me what you think, or just read a few.

I love reading work from other aspiring writers, too. Drop me a line if you’re interested in a critique session.
